Acme reviews the operation of this clause not less frequently than once
every twelve (12) months and updates the policy text where the review
identifies a material gap. Acme shall conduct hardship assessment in
accordance with the principles set out in this section.

Compliance with this clause is monitored through the quarterly second-line
assurance programme and reported to the Risk Committee. If a customer's
circumstances change materially after the assessment, Acme will, on
becoming aware of the change, repeat the relevant inquiries to the extent
required by the internal Acme credit-risk standard. An exception to this
clause may be granted by the Privacy Officer on a case-by-case basis,
provided the rationale is recorded in writing. Failure to comply with this
clause is a reportable matter under the Internal Compliance Reporting
Procedure and may attract disciplinary action. Acme will, where reasonably
practicable, communicate the application of this clause to the customer in
plain English at the relevant decision point.
