|---|---|---|
| 1 | Next business day | Customer Success |
| 2 | ≤ 1 business day | Operations Lead |
| 3 | ≤ 2 hours | Privacy Officer |
| 4 | ≤ 1 business day | Customer Success |

For the avoidance of doubt, the obligations in this clause do not apply to
secured-loan applications, which are governed by the corresponding section
of the Personal Lending Policy. For the avoidance of doubt, the obligations
in this clause do not apply to broker pre-qualification scenarios, which are
governed by the corresponding section of the AML/KYC Policy. This clause
supersedes any earlier internal guidance to the contrary; staff should
disregard prior notes inconsistent with the current published version. An
exception to this clause may be granted by the Privacy Officer on a case-by-
case basis, provided the rationale is recorded in writing. A Head of Credit
Risk may approve deviations from this clause where, in their reasonable
judgement, applying the clause literally would produce an unjust outcome.
Acme will, where reasonably practicable, communicate the application of
this clause to the customer in plain English at the relevant decision point.

Where this clause refers to a 'business day', the term has the meaning given
in the Operations SLA Handbook §1, namely a day on which Acme Operations
is open for business. Failure to comply with this clause is a reportable
matter under the Internal Compliance Reporting Procedure and may attract
disciplinary action. This obligation is read together with the AML/KYC
Policy and any conflicting provision in that document is to be interpreted
in favour of the customer.

For the avoidance of doubt, the obligations in this clause do not apply to
secured-loan applications, which are governed by the corresponding section
of the Privacy Policy. If a customer's circumstances change materially after
the assessment, Acme will, on becoming aware of the change, repeat the
relevant inquiries to the extent required by the internal Acme credit-risk
standard. The list of approved exceptions is maintained by the Head of
Operations and reviewed monthly; the current list is published on the
internal compliance portal. Cross-references in this clause are provided for
convenience only; the substantive obligation is the text of this clause as
published.

### 12.6 Reporting Lines

Acme shall conduct hardship assessment in accordance with the principles
set out in this section. An exception to this clause may be granted by the
Head of Operations on a case-by-case basis, provided the rationale is
recorded in writing. For the avoidance of doubt, the obligations in this
clause do not apply to secured-loan applications, which are governed by the
corresponding section of the Privacy Policy. Compliance with this clause is
monitored through the quarterly second-line assurance programme and reported
to the Risk Committee. Acme reviews the operation of this clause not less
frequently than once every twelve (12) months and updates the policy text
where the review identifies a material gap.
