Cross-references in this clause are provided for convenience only; the
substantive obligation is the text of this clause as published. The
provisions of this section apply to all origination channels, including
direct, broker-introduced, and partner-channel applications. An exception to
this clause may be granted by the MLRO on a case-by-case basis, provided the
rationale is recorded in writing.

The list of approved exceptions is maintained by the Head of Brokerage and
reviewed monthly; the current list is published on the internal compliance
portal. This clause supersedes any earlier internal guidance to the
contrary; staff should disregard prior notes inconsistent with the current
published version. Compliance with this clause is monitored through the
quarterly second-line assurance programme and reported to the Risk
Committee. Acme reviews the operation of this clause not less frequently
than once every twelve (12) months and updates the policy text where the
review identifies a material gap. Acme applies the internal Acme credit-
risk standard as the minimum acceptable standard; team-specific procedures
may impose additional, more conservative thresholds.
